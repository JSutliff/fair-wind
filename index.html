About Us

Fair Wind Inspections is a complete home inspection company specializing in residential property inspections and wind mitigation inspections in the Tampa Bay Area. Inspecting in all cities in Pinellas, Pasco and Hillsborough Counties including Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Largo and New Port Richey.

We also conduct insurance inspections such as four point and Citizens roof condition certification inspections. We are a licensed, state certified and insured residential building contractor and home inspector who has been performing work in the Tampa Bay area for over 5 years. Our primary goal is to educate and inform our clients. There are multiple ways in which we achieve this goal, from walk through inspections to a full 40+ page report with photographs and descriptions as well as the wealth of information located here on our website. We strive to provide an excellent experience at a competitive price to our home buyers, sellers, insurance agents and realtors. Please feel free to explore the website at your leisure and as always if you have any questions

An inspection to determine the approximate remaining useful life left on a roof. Citizens Insurance of Florida is now requiring this inspection on roofs over 25 years old. Other insurance companies may begin to require it as well.
