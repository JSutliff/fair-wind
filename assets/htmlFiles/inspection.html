Home Inspections

Price: $250 under 1800 sq. Ft.

Technically exhaustive non-invasive inspection usually performed for new home buyers so they may better understand what they are getting into when buying a home.

Know What You're Buying

During this time we look at all aspects of the home to determine what condition each of its features are in. We will then provide you with an accurate and unbiased assessment of the property.

Full Color Report

This report is made up of 15 sections, including kitchen, bathrooms, heat/AC, electrical, roof, exterior areas and more. Within these 15 sections there are over 200 individual items which we evaluate thoroughly.

If you are present during the inspection we can walk you through and point out any major findings and answer any questions you may have. Education and experience is our best tool and it is our duty to relay that information to you the client, in a clear and detailed way so that you may make an informed decision.

The price for a home inspection in the Tampa Bay area starts at $250 for a home under 1800 sq. ft. If the home is larger or you need additional services along with the home inspection please call.
