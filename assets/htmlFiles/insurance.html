Save Money Now

Potentially save thousands of dollars on your home owners insurance!

We perform a wind mitigation inspection so that you can save money on your homeowners insurance. It’s quite simple and in less than an hour you could be saving up to 86% on the wind portion of your homeowners insurance. This can also be known as the hurricane or windstorm portion of your policy. Depending on where you are located and who your insurance provider is, the windstorm portion of your insurance can be nearly 70% of your total homeowners insurance policy. Let’s do some math. If your paying $2,000 a year for homeowners insurance and 70% of that is windstorm premium, that’s $1,400. If you meet specific criteria, up to 86% of that amount can be deducted. That’s a saving of $1,204 per year!

Save Up To 86%

The hurricane or windstorm portion makes up a large part of many policies potentially allowing for big savings!

In 2007 the Florida Legislature approved an amendment to §627.0629 (1) which stated “A rate filing for residential property insurance must include actuarially reasonable discounts, credits, or other rate differentials, or appropriate reductions in deductibles, for properties on which fixtures or construction techniques demonstrated to reduce the amount of loss in a windstorm have been installed or implemented.”
